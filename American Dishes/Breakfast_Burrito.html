Eggscellent Recipes

Cooking Tips

  • Put bacon into cold oven before heating
  • For soft scrambled eggs, constantly stir eggs with a spatchula
  • Add jalapeno to eggs or hot sauce to burrito to increase the spiciness
  • Cook the onions in the rendered chorizo fat

Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 1 large tortilla
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 Tater Tots
  • 1 quarter of a yellow onion
  • 1 quarter cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup of chorizo

Instructions

  1. Turn an oven on to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Right afterwards, add the 2 strips of bacon into the oven and cook until desired donness
  3. Heat a pan up to medium heat, then add in chorizo
  4. Cook chorizo on pan for 15-20 minutes
  5. Remove the chorizo from the pan
  6. Dice up quarter of an onion
  7. Add to saucepan and keep on medium heat for 5 minutes
  8. Bring saucepan down to low heat
  9. Mix the 3 eggs well
  10. Add eggs to saucepan with onions and continously mix the eggs
  11. Once the eggs are set but still slightly watery, remove from saucepan
  12. Cook the 6 tater tots by following instructions on the bag
  13. Place the tortilla on a plate
  14. Spread sour cream all over the tortilla
  15. Add tater tots, eggs, cheddar cheese, chorizo, and bacon to tortilla
  16. Finish up and enjoy!
